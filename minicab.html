MINI AND TAXI CAB SERVICES

IT Logi is a taxi staffing service, which offers HR outsourcing and other services for the convenience of the taxi companies. We mainly function as a customer communication management system specifically designed to effectively bridge the gap between the minicab firms and their passengers. Our aim is to allow Minicab firms to take control of the opportunities offered by the market to ensure a smooth functioning across the city through the taxi dispatch system.

Controllers

Controllers are the lifeline of the taxi dispatch system as they are equipped with the skills to use the information given by the telephonists in order to manage entire fleets of drivers in the most efficient way possible. Our expert team of controllers is trained specifically to manage drivers and to align their schedules as per the needs and requirements that a cab company faces on a daily basis. Our hardworking and trustworthy controllers can make driver management easier for even the largest fleets in the UK. Besides, we train the controllers according to the specific requirements of minicab companies as well as their future plans of growth and expansion.

Live Chat

Communication forms the crux of all the successful working systems. Along with 24x7 call-based services for all types of minicab firms, we also have Live Chat sessions through which passenger communication can be made convenient like never before.

Online portals

Online portals take in more intricate information from the passengers to meet their specified requirements. The skill-sets of our team of telephonists and controllers are nurtured in such a way that management of such type of data is done in a professionally adept manner. This allows us to communicate even the most specific and detailed passenger requests in the most convenient and rapid way possible.

CLI Promotional Sheets

Passenger data analytics and management is our forte. We have a special team of individuals who create customized CLI promotional sheets on a periodic basis for your Minicab firm, inclusive of information such as usual pick up and drop locations, most preferred time of travel, number of cancellations and more.

Driver Earning Sheets

From total number of trips and daily earnings to amount to be deposited in the account and incentives for the month, a clear and well-defined data set of informational statistics on drivers is what we offer when it comes to managing your fleet in the best way possible.
